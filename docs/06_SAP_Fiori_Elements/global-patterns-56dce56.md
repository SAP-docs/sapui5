# Global Patterns

Global patterns provide capabilities for the whole app, not just individual building blocks.

Global patterns are app-level capabilities delivered by SAP Fiori elements. They provide out-of-the-box, first-class integration with a variety of services such as AI capabilities, SAP Fiori launchpad, and OData services. This means you don't have to build these capabilities from scratch.

Some of these features are enabled automatically depending on what is available in your environment. You can configure others in the `manifest.json` file, either manually or using [SAP Fiori tools](https://help.sap.com/viewer/product/SAP_FIORI_tools/Latest/en-US). The framework renders the UI and applies the behavior consistently across all relevant pages and floorplans.
